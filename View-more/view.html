About Me

I’m a 19-year-old aspiring photographer from Cabuyao, Laguna. I love capturing clean, striking shots that tell stories—whether it’s portraits, landscapes, or candid street moments. Recently, I’ve been exploring editing styles and experimenting with lighting to give my photos a unique mood and depth.

My goal is to become a skilled photographer who captures not just images, but emotions—creating photographs that inspire and connect with people.
